The Ideal User Journey

A step-by-step breakdown of how users interact with the Speech Accessibility App

1User Opens the App

When the user opens the app, they are presented with a simple, clean interface with two primary options:

  • Select words manually from a list (for non-verbal users)
  • Speak into the app (for users with unclear speech)

The interface is designed to be accessible and intuitive, requiring minimal setup or configuration.

2User Speaks or Selects Words

Depending on the user's abilities and preferences:

  • If they speak, the app analyzes their speech in real-time using ASR enhancement technology
  • The app suggests words or phrases based on what it understands
  • If they select words manually, the app provides contextually relevant options
  • The AI middleware predicts likely next words to speed up communication

3AI Learns & Improves Over Time

The app employs sophisticated learning algorithms to adapt to the user:

  • Remembers how the user talks, even if their speech is unclear
  • Builds a personalized speech model for each user
  • Learns common phrases, word choices, and communication patterns
  • Adapts to changes in the user's speech over time
  • Improves prediction accuracy with continued use

4App Converts Text to Speech

The final step in the communication process:

  • The app speaks the selected or recognized words out loud
  • Uses Smart TTS technology to generate natural-sounding speech
  • Speech output sounds like the user's own voice (after AI training)
  • Maintains the user's identity through personalized voice characteristics
  • Delivers real-time, fluid communication in social settings

Core Technology Components

The three key technological pillars that power the Speech Accessibility App

Speech Normalization (ASR Enhancement)

Advanced speech recognition technology specifically designed for users with speech impairments:

  • Processes unclear or atypical speech patterns
  • Filters out background noise and speech artifacts
  • Adapts to individual speech characteristics
  • Improves recognition accuracy over time
  • Works with various speech disorders and conditions
  • Provides real-time feedback and suggestions

AI-Powered Middleware

Intelligent prediction and processing system that bridges input and output:

  • Suggests words and phrases based on context
  • Learns from user selections and corrections
  • Predicts likely next words to speed up communication
  • Understands user's common topics and vocabulary
  • Adapts to different communication scenarios
  • Provides contextually relevant options
  • Reduces the cognitive load on users

Smart TTS (Speech Output)

Advanced text-to-speech technology that preserves the user's identity:

  • Generates natural-sounding speech from text
  • Creates a personalized voice model based on the user's speech
  • Maintains the user's vocal identity and characteristics
  • Adjusts tone, pitch, and emphasis for natural communication
  • Supports multiple languages and accents
  • Provides real-time, fluid communication in social settings
  • Allows users to "speak" even when they cannot physically do so
